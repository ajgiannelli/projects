Coach's Note
Your fitness is not the question. VO2 of 61 and interval work down to sub-5:00 pace means the engine is there. The variable is execution — specifically, how disciplined you are in the first 13 miles when everything feels easy and every instinct says push.

Pittsburgh punishes front-runners. The climbs at miles 3–4 and 10–11 will cost you HR spikes if you're not deliberate. Bank the discipline, not the seconds. Every mile you run at 6:20 when the plan says 6:27 is a debt you pay after mile 20.

Run 6:30–6:35 through mile 8. Be patient. Lock 6:27 miles 9–20. Then at mile 20, if you've protected the effort, you have something left. That's the race. Everything before mile 20 is setup.
— Race Plan, Pittsburgh 2026
